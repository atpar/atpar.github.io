Services

All-Inclusive issuance, servicing and reporting services

Issuance

The journey of each and every security starts with drafting a term sheet reflecting the terms & conditions of your offering.

We help you save time and cost by utilizing standard, machine-readable product templates from which digital securities can be issued by the push of a button.

Record keeping

For maximum data integrity, record your securities in our fully digital and DLT-powered securities register using term sheets.

We deploy a dedicated digital securities register for every issuer so that you can retain full control over your securities.

Reporting

Don’t get lost in the data jungle anymore. Let our reporting services create standard reports for you.

Configure the report schema, and we’ll crunch the numbers. A perfect division of labour.

Corporate actions

Automate processing your corporate actions with our built-in calculating and paying agent services.

The upcoming actions are projected directly from the security’s term sheet requiring minimal interaction. Our paying agent services allow for an efficient distribution of payments (interest, dividends, principal, etc.) among investors.

Audit trail

How did we get to where we are?

Never stay in the dark about your past activity again. Always maintain a full audit trail evidencing the current state of your records.

Event Subscriptions

Anytime. Anywhere. On any device. Use our configurable event subscription service and stay on top of what’s happening with your securities.

Simply fetch the current event queue or feed your systems and processes with such event notifications.

Platform

Next-generation capital markets infrastructure
